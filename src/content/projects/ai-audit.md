## About the Project

With the rise of AI search engines and chatbots, many websites are losing visibility. Companies don't know how their pages appear in AI answers and are losing customers.

### Solution

The tool audits websites from an AI perspective — checking structure, metadata, schema markup, and simulating how content will be interpreted by AI systems. Output includes specific recommendations and exports.

### Features

- AI answer visibility analysis
- Schema markup and structured data check
- Problematic pattern detection
- Export recommendations to CSV/PDF/XML
- Competitor comparison
